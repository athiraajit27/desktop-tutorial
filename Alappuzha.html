Alappuzha

Alappuzha (Alleppey) is known as ‘the Venice of the East’. Offering the best tourist places in Alleppey, this charming place is the hub of Kerala’s backwaters and is home to a huge network of backwaters and more than thousand houseboats.

Vembanadu Lake

Famed as the longest lake in India, Vembanad Lake flaunts its enchanting beauty across several places in Kerala. However, while romancing the Alleppey Backwaters, you can enjoy the best of while relaxing in a houseboat.

Marari Beach

A perfect escapade for all the holiday mongers, Marari Beach (or Mararikulam Beach) showcases an ideal mix of leisure, fun, excitement, peace, and serenity. Home to some of the world-class Ayurvedic centres in India, this beach can keep you well-introduced with the vastness of the Arabian Sea and its swaying beauty!

Kuttanadu

A picturesque hamlet that spreads across both Alleppey and Kottayam district, Kuttanad is also known as the 'Rice Bowl of Kerala' due its vast paddy cultivation. But the fact that Kuttanad is among the only few places in the entire globe where cultivation is actually done below the sea level, makes it a much more fascinating place to visit while on a Kerala tour.
