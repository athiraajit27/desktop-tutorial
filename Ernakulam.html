Ernakulam

Ernakulam is the commercial capital of the Southern Indian State of Kerala, is sometimes referred to as the 'Queen of the Arabian Sea'.Ernakulam proudly boasts a rich cultural heritage, but is one of the most fast-paced and modern cities in India. It has one of the finest natural harbours of the world and a major Indian port.

Vypeen Island

Vypeen is one of the popular places to visit in Ernakulam in one day and certainly one of the best islands in Kerala. A short and beautiful ferry ride from the city will take to this serene island which is dotted with beaches, backwaters and an array of restaurants and hotels.

Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary

One of the first bird sanctuaries in Kerala and undeniably a haven for nature enthusiasts. In the words of Salim Ali (world famous ornithologist), Thattekkad is the “richest bird sanctuary in India” since it is home to hundreds of different endangered species of migratory birds.

Cherai Beach

Located on the north end of Vypin island in Kochi, Cherai beach is a 15 km stretch of wild and rugged beauty. Stunning lagoons, wide paddy fields and coconut grooves adorn the road to the beach.
